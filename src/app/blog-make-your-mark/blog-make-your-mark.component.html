Make your mark at K 2025 with Avila

June 28, 2023 By Avila International

The number one trade fair for the plastics and rubber industry in the whole of the world, K is scheduled for 2025 and Avila Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd is ready with a meticulously planned K 2025 travel package for you!

Pioneering innovations, developments, visionary impulses, research, future perspectives and solutions; expect all this and more at K 2025, world's number one trade fair for the plastics and rubber industry. The fair is scheduled to be held from 8th October to 15th October 2025 in Düsseldorf, Germany.

We know you can't wait and we're just as thrilled for you. That's why team Avila has rolled out the K 2025 travel package - to make your trip to Düsseldorf, Germany a fulfilling and memorable one.

Here's how you can completely entrust Avila Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd with your travel responsibilities and aspirations.

1. Get started with booking form

The first step to making your K 2025 journey happen is by getting registered via our booking form and making the initial payments. Ensure you conform with all the relevant documents as per the rules of the respective embassy or consulate, who will be the decider in granting the visa. If by any chance your visa isn't approved, you can initiate a registration termination in line with our cancellation policy. Connect with us to know more!

2. Flights and transportation

All your flights and tour-related transportation requirements, especially to the expo site in Düsseldorf, will be completely arranged by Avila Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd. Our team will make sure you journey at ease every step of the way.

3. Accommodation in Düsseldorf

Finding a suitable accommodation in a city you don't know much about can be nerve-racking. But imagine not having to worry about this at all. Avila’s established connections with renowned hotel chains ensure you are accommodated in a hotel within proximity to the expo venue.

4. Meal packages

Your breakfasts will be provided by the hotel. Dinners and lunches will be arranged by team Avila, with the dinners comprising Indian cuisine while lunches will be packed and carried to the expo.

5. Round-the-clock support

From unforeseen emergencies to queries and changes, Avila's dedicated team is just a call away, to ensure your mind is fully involved in the K 2025 experience without having to worry over anything.

With decades of experience planning and managing international trips of all kinds, especially for world renowned trade expos and other events, there's nothing Avila Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd can't do for you. Read more about our package and clauses here. If you're interested in joining our K 2025 expedition, connect with us now!
