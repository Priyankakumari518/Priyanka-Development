Explore the Labelexpo Europe 2023 experience with Avila

June 26, 2023 By Avila International

World's largest event for the label and package printing industry is taking place at Brussels in September and team Avila is all geared to make your trip successful in every sense.

The city of Brussels is gearing up to host one of the world’s largest events and welcome trade visitors from across the globe. Touted as the most important trade fair for labels, package printing and production technology, Labelexpo Europe is a golden opportunity for the professionals in the industry and is held biennially.

If you belong to the industry, we know you wouldn’t want to miss the opportunity to take part in the expo. And that’s why Avila Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd is here to plan and manage the trip down to the last detail for you. Soak in the Labelexpo Europe 2023 experience without any hiccups, while we pick up the rest. Here’s how!

5 ways to make your travels sustainable
1. The onboarding process

You get registered for the tour once you fill our booking form and make the necessary payments. Passengers will have to comply with all the relevant documents as per the rules of the respective embassy or consulate, who will be the decider in granting the visa. In case of non-availability of visa, passengers can cancel the tour as per the cancellation policy which will be provided along with the booking form. Feel free to contact us to know more!

2. Seamless transportation

Trust our team to find the most convenient flight options that align with your schedule and preferences. More transportation requirements like air ground transfers to and fro, transfer from airport to hotel and back, will all be flawlessly handled by team Avila. Sit back and rest assured that you’ll reach your destinations on time, conveniently.

3. Safe & comfortable accommodation

Securing a well-suited accommodation in Brussels during a big event can feel like a daunting task; but not if you are with Avila Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd. Our long-standing and established partnerships mean your accommodation needs are completely taken care of, letting you focus on the expo and the enormous networking opportunities awaiting you.

4. Food arrangement

The only thing you are going to be hungry for is the exposure and opportunities you will be getting at Labelexpo Europe 2023. While the hotel will be providing breakfasts, dinners and packed lunches at the fairground will be arranged by our team.

5. 24/7 customer support

Apart from all these, our team will be available with round-the-clock customer support, ensuring that you have assistance whenever you need it. As you expand horizons, showcase your skill and gain as much insights as possible. We will make sure the journey is stress-free and enjoyable.

Avila Tours & Travels Pvt Ltd looks forward to being your reliable partner in your Labelexpo Europe 2023 experience. As you benefit from the incredible opportunity to stay ahead in the label and package printing industry, our team, with the meticulously planned travel packages, hassle-free accommodation, convenient flight bookings, ensure the trip is every bit successful and memorable. Contact now to get registered!
